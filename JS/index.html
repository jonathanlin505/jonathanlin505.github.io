Hello

This is the Pokemon Team Manager for generations 1 through 6. This tool is used to track team type advantages and weaknesses for competitive play. See below to see how to use this website.

How to Use

  1. To start, head over to the Build page.
  2. Search the Pokemon you want on your team using or . You can search by either Pokemon name, dex number, or type (note that you cannot search by both name/dex no. and type).
  3. Scroll down to see the results of your search. Click to add Pokemon in your team.
  4. Pokemon that you add to your team will appear in your party, displayed on the top of the page. To remove Pokemon, either click to remove Pokemon individually or click to remove your entire team.
  5. Once you're satisfied with your team, click to have your team evaluated.
