How to read the charts

The type matchup charts assess the strengths of your team based on the number of Pokemon on your team and the types of your Pokemon. The scales of both charts range from 0 (no advantage) to 100 (absolute advantage). For instance, a 0 indicates no damage dealt against a certain type and taken from a certain type in offense and defense respectively, whereas a 100 indicates 4x effectiveness in the same regard.
